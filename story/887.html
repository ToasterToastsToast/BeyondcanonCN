Swiss: Examine bookshelf.

You hustle downstairs to your PERSONAL LIBRARIUM, which you keep stocked full of CLASSICAL TEXTS, DECREES and DOCTRINES, many of which outline the core tenets of COMBAT, PHILOSOPHY, and, you know, the other two. Satyrical society hinges on the twin principles of MERIT and FRATERNITY, and as such you've taken it upon yourself to ensure your education is as comprehensively stacked as your social life. To be blunt, you are always on that grind.
