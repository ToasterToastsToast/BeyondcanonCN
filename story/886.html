They're knocking quite loudly...

Perhaps you've led them TOO well, but they won't be able to rely on you forever. What will these frosh do when you graduate? Honeyfall will always be your beloved ALMA PATER, but you can't mill around this academic middleground forever; you've got more momentous moves on the horizon.
