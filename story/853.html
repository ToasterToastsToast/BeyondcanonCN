Ly'lac: Examine drawing.

This is a drawing you made of two of your favorite cartoon characters. You took some creative liberties with one of the designs here though, namely making the main character a nymph. Though it's a little embarrassing to admit, you've always felt that he was a very nymphlike character especially given how mercurial his body was. There was a time when you wondered if the creator was a nymph themself, but it turned out they were just some old satyr from a bajillion tides ago. When you searched the othernet for discussion on the topic it seemed no one felt the same. You didn't get along with people on one of the major fansites either. You argued with users saying "He didn't really act like a nymph." and "If there was any character that could be a nymph it'd be the blue one." To which you thought: Yeah sure, you guess? You never really understood why that was an acceptable exception to the rule. They're all just colorful pieces of clay. You haven't really thought about this since you were banned from the fansite for "ip spoofing" and "mailing bombs" to various users' "homes". This subject always manages to stress you out. You crumple up the drawing and eat it.
