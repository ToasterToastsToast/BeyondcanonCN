Hours ago, but not many.

Midnight City. Where the nights never end. Where the black edges of the sky fade into the seething darkness of the rippling ocean, and the void washes up on the shore like waves. Midnight city, where the only thing darker than its name are the hearts of the people living inside it. Midnight City. Where it's always midnight. Actually, that's not true. It's other times too, it just sounds cool when you say it like that.
