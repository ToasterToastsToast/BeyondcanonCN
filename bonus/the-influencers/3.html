PRINCIPAL: You three were there.

SILAS: I don't recall--

PRINCIPAL: Don't try and worm out of this, Miss Beauregard. I have ears all over.

PRINCIPAL: You were there, and you saw everything happen. And you're going to fill me in on all the details.

PRINCIPAL: I have to calm down the news ships and Crockercorp representatives first, so wait for me in my office. I'll be right there after I clean up this P.R. nightmare and we can all work out the correct version of the story, do I make myself clear?

He looks not at all bothered by the supposed P.R. nightmare as he smooths his hair back and turns to walk toward the camera crews.

As Imode, Silas, and Avril make their slow way toward their punitive fate, politely avoiding eye contact with one another, they hear their principal address the rest of their cohort.

PRINCIPAL: Everybody else stay put until I say so.
