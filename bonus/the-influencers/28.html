The trio take a moment to experience a collective traumatic flashback of the hour or so they spent beneath the shadow of Strider Memorial Tower. They'd scarcely unlocked it when the four main characters had come hustling across the field, and the only place to hide in a hurry had been the other side of the tower. They'd huddled together, hoping that no one would think to scout out the area before ascending. Luckily, they weren't dealing with professionals.
