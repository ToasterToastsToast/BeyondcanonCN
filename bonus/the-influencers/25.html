The Influencers, Part 3

As anyone acquainted with the practice will know, facilitating canon is hungry work. Even if that work only consists of unlocking a door and failing to testify in front of a news camera, therefore refusing to assist in the brutal and obvious railroading of several (potentially) innocent main characters. It still takes a good dose of "moral fiber," as Avril's aunt might put it. She likes cop shows and dramas with quick and dirty storylines that can be wrapped up in 48 minutes, with no doubt as to who the villain is. She'd probably be totally gutted to know that Avril gave up the chance to bring some (potential) criminals to justice.
